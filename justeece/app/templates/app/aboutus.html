How It Started

It is true that more and more sole proprietors and small businesses are now going online to find their customers. Also, more and more people are now finding fulltime, parttime and even freelance gigs online. There are lots of good people who are offering and delivering legitimate services online. Unfortunately, there are also some bad actors who breach contractual agreements.

In 2018 when we started hiring remote workers, we realized that employees are more cautious of the behavior if they know that whatever they do can be traced back to them in the future. On the other hand, if there are no consequences for bad behavior, remote employees do not honor company agreements and rules of good business conduct.
